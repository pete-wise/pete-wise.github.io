Snickerdoodle

Ingredients

  • 1 cup vegetable shortening
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 teaspoons cream of tartar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon table salt
  • 2 3/4 cups all purpose flour
  • 1/2 cups cinnamon sugar

Baking Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. Lightly grease two baking sheets
  3. Beat together the shortneing and sugar until smooth, then beat in the eggs, again beating until smooth.
  4. Mix in the vanilla, cream of tartar, baking soda, and salt, then add the flour. Mix until combined.
  5. Place the cinnamon sugar in a 8" round cake pan
  6. Drop the dough by tablespoon into the pan with the sugar. Gently shake the pan to coat the balls with sugar.
  7. Place the cookies on the prepared baking sheets, leaving about 1 1/2" between them. Using the bottom of a glass, flatten each cookie until it is about 1/2" thick.
  8. Bake the cookies for 11-12 minutes.
  9. Remove the cookies from the oven once they begin to turn golden. Transfer them to a rack to cool.
