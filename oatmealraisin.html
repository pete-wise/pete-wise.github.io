Oatmeal Raisin

Ingredients

  • 8 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/3 cup light brown sugar or dark brown sugar, packed to taste
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon allspice
  • 1/2 teaspoon ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon table salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons honey
  • 3/4 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups quick oats
  • 1 1/2 cups raisins

Baking Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
  2. Line two baking sheets with parchment paper.
  3. Mix together the butter, sugars, spices, baking soda, and vanilla until fully combined.
  4. Mix in the eggs, and then the honey.
  5. Stir in the flour, then the oats and raisins.
  6. Cover the dough and refrigerate for 1.5 hours, until chilled.
  7. Drop tablespoon-sized dough balls onto baking sheets, and leave 2" between them.
  8. Bake cookies for 10 minutes, until they just begin to brown.
  9. Remove the cookies from the oven, and let them sit to cool.
