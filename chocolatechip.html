Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup light brown sugar
  • 2/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 8 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup vegatable shortening
  • 3/4 teapsoon table salt
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon cider vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Baking Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. Mix the usgars, butter, shortening, salt, vanilla, almond extract, vinegar, and baking soda until smooth.
  3. Mix in the egg until completely smooth and combined.
  4. Mix in the flour and chocoalte chips.
  5. Scoop out 1 1/4" balls of dough onto the prepared baking sheets, leaving around 2" of space between each one.
  6. Add a sprinkle of sea salt atop the balls of dough.
  7. Bake the cookies for 12 minutes, until the tops are golden brown.
  8. Remove the cookies from the oven and set down to cool.
